# Spec-Driven Development

## Details

### How did you structure your spec for Kiro to implement?

Our spec was structured with three Markdown files: a design, requirements, and tasks. The design detailed a general overview of the app, the architecture and technical choices made and the reasons for them, and the components used and other system design. The requirements were the raw requirements necessary to get the app to work; what parts of the design are needed, and how should they flow and be implemented? It can be read as a summary of the design. The tasks were the steps that the agent took to implement the design and requirements; creating directories and editing files to fulfill each part of the overall spec.

### How did the spec-driven approach improve your development process? How did this compare to vibe coding?

Designing the MVP this way as opposed to doing it conversationally helped reduce the number of errors and the vagueness of the project’s design. If we hadn’t done this and had stuck to vibe coding, the project would’ve likely been less organized and confusing to develop for.
